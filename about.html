About

Fareeha Nezam is a first-year college student pursuing a BA program with a major in Computer Science and a non-major in Mathematics from the University of Delhi. As a highly motivated and ambitious individual, Fareeha is determined to excel in her academic pursuits and acquire the skills necessary to succeed in the field of technology.

She works as a staff writer for Periphery Magazine, a non-profit online publication that focuses on highlighting stories of underrepresented voices. She is also an active member of Girl Up, a global leadership development initiative, and works with an impact startup called "Plug in the World" to bring about positive change through her contributions.

Recently, she has taken on a new challenge by starting a computer science internship with a global development community program. She believes that this opportunity will help give her career a kick start by allowing her to gain practical experience in the tech industry.

In addition to her work and studies,she enjoys reading novels, writing poetry and articles, and staying up to date on the latest developments in technology and innovation.

Looking ahead, Fareeha's professional goals include building a strong foundation of technical skills, gaining experience in the tech industry, and making a positive impact on society through her work. With her dedication and commitment to excellence, she is poised to achieve these objectives and become a leading figure in the field of computer science.
